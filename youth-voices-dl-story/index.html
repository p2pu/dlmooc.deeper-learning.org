Youth Voices

Posted on December 27, 2013 by Karen Fasimpaur

(This is a part of the Deeper Learning Story Bank. Add your story!)

Story title: Youth Voices

Submitted by: Karen Fasimpaur

DL story: Youth Voices is an online site for conversations. We invite youth of all ages to voice their thoughts about their passions, to explain things they understand well, to wonder about things they have just begun to understand, and to share discussion posts with other young people using as many different genres and media as they can imagine.

Categories:
Collaborating; Self-directing learning; Doing relevant, engaging work; Making things, Presenting work to a real audience
