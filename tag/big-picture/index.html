Wk. 3 email – Students in the adult world

Posted on February 2, 2014 by Karen Fasimpaur
Reply

(This is a duplicate of the Feb. 2 email sent to registered participants.)

Dear DLMOOC community,

Welcome to week 3 of DLMOOC, where we’ll be looking at students in the adult world, internships, real world learning, and more. All the activities for the week are on the web site.

  • We’ll be having another great panel discussion this Monday, Feb. 3 at 4pm Pacific (Los Angeles). This one will feature Elliot Washor, Big Picture Learning; Tim Jenkins, Principal, MetEast School; Joe Battaglia, Director of Curriculum & Instruction at The Met in Providence; and a mentor and student from a Big Picture school.
  • The “Lens in the Classroom” live event will be held this Thursday, Feb. 6 at 4pm Pacific (Los Angeles). In this we’ll be helping Randy Scherer with his dilemma surrounding internships.
  • This week, we have more resources to spark your thinking, including videos with students from San Diego Met, part of the Big Picture network and High Tech High.

As always, feel free to alter these activities to meet your own learning needs and continue the conversations in your groups and with your peers.

Keep those comments, questions, and suggestions coming! That’s how we all learn.

Sincerely,

Ben, Rob, Laura, Ryan, Karen, and the whole DLMOOC team
