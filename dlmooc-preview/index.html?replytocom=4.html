DLMOOC preview

The DLMOOC Academic Mindsets preview week has now concluded. If you missed it, all the action is archived here.
—————————

As you may know, we are hosting a Deeper Learning MOOC starting in January 2014. Although we often say planning is for pessimists, we are hoping to pilot a part of the course now while offering a meaningful experience to our friends.

To that end, we are hosting a pilot session for the course the week of November 4-8, 2013. The focus of this preview is on Academic Mindsets.

What:
Preview Session for DL MOOC focusing the use of Academic Mindsets.

When: Nov 4-8, 2013

Dates:
November 4 at 4pm PST: Academic Mindset Panel
November 6 at 4pm: Tuning Protocol on Academic Mindsets

Note that these sessions will be hosted on a Google hangout. Participants may engage in the hangouts in real-time or participate on their schedule. It would be beneficial to have participants in both configurations.

We hope you will be part of this extra special group!
