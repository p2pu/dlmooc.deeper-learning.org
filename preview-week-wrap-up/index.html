Academic Mindsets Preview Week Wrap-up

Posted on November 8, 2013 by Karen Fasimpaur

What a great week it’s been! Thank you to everyone who participated.

No matter how you participated, we’d greatly appreciate it if you’d complete this very quick survey to help us make DLMOOC even better!

If you want to catch up on this week’s activities, we’ve archived the Academic Mindsets panel, the consultancy protocol, and other content on our web site. We’ve also storified the week here.

Make sure to sign up for DLMOOC in January and spread the word to your friends. In the meantime, stay in touch with us and talk more about deeper learning on our G+ community and on Twitter.

Hope to see you soon online, and we can’t wait for January!
